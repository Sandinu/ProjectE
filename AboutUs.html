About.Project.E

Project E is an Initiative by the Interact Club of Mahanama College with the aim of making a fast and proper path between human and the knowledge about these very special Endemic animals of Sri Lanka.

Who.Are.We?

We are the Interact Club of Mahanama College, Colombo-03. ICMC is an Interact club under the Interact District 3220 which was chartered in 1989 with the sponsoring Rotary Club of Down Town. Project-E, the biggest virtual awareness project about Endemic animals of Sri Lanka is a project carried out by the 2021/2022 board of the ICMC.
